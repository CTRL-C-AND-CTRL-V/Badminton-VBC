About the Centre

A home for the community, built for the game.

Victoria Badminton Centre is Melbourne's most ambitious badminton facility — a purpose-built venue designed around one simple belief: great badminton deserves great space.

From families picking up a racquet for the first time to elite players training for national competition, our 36 courts across two sports — 28 badminton (including 2 SVIP) and 8 pickleball — professional lighting, and sprung sports flooring create the conditions for your best game.

Pro Lighting
Shadow-free LED rigs tuned for shuttlecock clarity.
Sprung Flooring
BWF-approved surfaces for performance and joint care.
Coaching
Accredited coaches, junior programs, adult clinics.
